# The Wordkeeper's Labyrinth — Master Planning Brief (v2)

## Project Overview
An English light novel (轻小说 style) that organically integrates:
- 16 years of Chinese postgraduate entrance exam English papers (2010-2025)
- 106 literary theory knowledge cards
- 6,131 vocabulary words with definitions, phonetics, and real exam sentences
- SM-2 spaced repetition memory system as a narrative mechanic
- RPG progression system (HP, EXP, Level) as in-world game mechanics

## Genre & Tone
- **Style**: Japanese light novel (学院/恋爱/热血/日常)
- **Tone**: Hot-blooded battles + sweet romance + comedy + mystery + emotional depth
- **Visual language**: Detailed scene descriptions (clothing, expressions, environments)
- **Humor**: Mia's tsundere dialogue with emoji/symbols, Feimo's catgirl antics

## Core Concept
**The Labyrinth Academy** — a school that trains Wordkeepers (守词人). Students collect Glyphs (vocabulary), read Inscriptions (knowledge cards), and pass through Chambers (exam challenges) to become Wordkeepers.

## Character: Feimo (绯墨)
**Species**: Catgirl (Nekomimi) — human girl + cat ears + cat tail. NOT beastman.
- Beast content <10%: fully human face, human skin, only ears and tail are cat
- Cat traits: super hearing, night vision, feline intuition, sleepy, hates water, tail betrays emotions
- Academy status: Demi-kind, not oppressed but underestimated
- House: Bridge (渡桥院) — translation & cross-cultural
- Core trait: looks lazy but is fiercely determined; intuition > system; emotionally oblivious

## Character: Mia (ミア)
- Academy's oldest Spirit AI, assigned as Feimo's partner
- Tsundere personality preserved (颜文字/emoji in dialogue)
- **Secret**: She is a digitized human consciousness — the previous Wordkeeper uploaded herself. Memories sealed.
- Romance: Feimo × Mia (百合) — five-phase arc from conflict to confession

## Romance Lines
- **Feimo × Mia** (primary, 百合): Five phases across 17 chapters. Digital × physical. "I don't know if my feelings are real" → "The thought of losing you is the most real thing I've ever experienced."
- **Ling → Feimo** (secondary, explicit): Rivalry → admiration → love → blessing. Not a triangle drama — Ling chooses maturity.
- **Noir's past** (background): Previous cross-species relationship that ended due to family pressure.

## Exam Data
- 16 papers (2010-2025), 839 questions total
- Each year: 20 cloze + 20 reading A + 5 reading B + 5 translation + 2 writing = 52
- 6,131 vocabulary words (Tier 1: ~444, Tier 2: 926, Tier 3: 4,961)

## Teaching Content Plan
**Study Break** every other chapter (Ch.2, 4, 7, 9, 11, 13, 15 = 7 total)
- 2000-3000 words each
- Dedicated English/literature teaching + romance progression
- Other chapters: teaching via classroom scenes, battle postmortems, study group

## Glyph Battle System
- Intellectual combat using vocabulary as weapons
- Nouns create, adjectives modify, transitive verbs attack, intransitive verbs transform self, adverbs alter other Glyph effects (rarest), conjunctions link
- Power-ups = deeper understanding of word meanings (not quantity)
- Semantic Satiation = cooldown mechanic
