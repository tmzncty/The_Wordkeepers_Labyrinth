## 答题区

### Q1. shop-floor lighting _1_ workers’ productivity

**选项**:
- [A] affected
- [B] achieved
- [C] extracted
- [D] restored

**答案**: A

**解析**: affected 影响。根据上下文，此处需要一个动词，表示车间照明如何影响工人的生产力。

---

### Q2. the studies ended _2_ giving their name to the “Hawthorne effect,”

**选项**:
- [A] at
- [B] up
- [C] with
- [D] off

**答案**: B

**解析**: end up doing sth 最终成为，最终处于某种状态，强调结果出人意料。

---

### Q3. the very _3_ of being experimented upon changed subjects’ behavior.

**选项**:
- [A] truth
- [B] sight
- [C] act
- [D] proof

**答案**: C

**解析**: act 行为。此处需要一个名词，表示“受试”这一行为本身改变了受试者的行为。

---

### Q4. The idea arose because of the _4_ behavior of the women in the plant.

- [C] mischievous
- [D] ambiguous

**解析**: perplexing 令人费解的。根据下文，女工的行为令人不解，因为无论照明增加还是减少，生产率都提高。
