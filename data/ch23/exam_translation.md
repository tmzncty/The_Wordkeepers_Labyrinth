# Ch. — 翻译 (Translation)

> **年份**: 2016 | **总分**: 10分

---

### Q46. We don’t have to learn how to be mentally healthy; it is built into us in the same way that our bodies know how to heal a cut or mend a broken bone.

**参考译文/解析**: 本句考查定语从句和被动语态。主句是被动句“it is built into us”，其中it指代mental health。 “in the same way that”引导定语从句，修饰way，意为“正如...一样”。

---

### Q47. Our mental health doesn’t really go anywhere; like the sun behind a cloud, it can be temporarily hidden from view, but it is fully capable of being restored in an instant.

**参考译文/解析**: 本句考查并列句和比喻。“like the sun behind a cloud”作状语，将心理健康比作云后的太阳。“be capable of being restored”是被动结构，可以翻译为主动形式“能够重现”。

---

### Q48. Mental health allows us to view others with sympathy if they are having troubles, with kindness if they are in pain, and with unconditional love no matter who they are.

**参考译文/解析**: 本句考查条件状语和固定搭配。“allows sb. to do sth.”意为“允许某人做某事”。“view…with…”意为“以...看待”。三个“with+抽象名词+if”的结构并列，表示在不同情况下，心理健康所带来的积极影响。“no matter who they are”引导让步状语从句。

---

### Q49. Although mental health is the cure-all for living our lives, it is perfectly ordinary as you will see that it has been there to direct you through all your difficult decisions.

**参考译文/解析**: 本句考查让步状语从句和宾语从句。“Although”引导让步状语从句。“as you will see”引导原因状语从句，可以翻译为“正如你将会看到的”。“that it has been there to direct you through all your difficult decisions”是see的宾语从句。

---

### Q50. As you will come to see, knowing that mental health is always available and knowing to trust it allow us to slow down to the moment and live life happily.

**参考译文/解析**: 本句考查时间状语从句和并列结构。“As you will come to see”引导时间状语从句。“knowing that mental health is always available and knowing to trust it”作主语，两个动名词短语并列。
