### Q46. yet when one looks at the photographs of the gardens created by the homeless, it strikes one that, for all their diversity of styles, these gardens speak of various other fundamental urges, beyond that of decoration  and creative expression.

**参考译文/解析**: 本句考查了状语从句、形式主语、省略结构和固定搭配。重点在于理解 it strikes one that... 的结构，以及 for all 的让步含义，和beyond的转折。

---

### Q47. A sacred place of peace, however crude it may be, is a distinctly human need, as opposed to shelter, which is a distinctly animal need.

**参考译文/解析**: 本句考查让步状语从句、固定搭配和非限制性定语从句。需要注意 however 引导的让步从句的用法，以及 as opposed to 的对比含义。distinctly用于修饰human和animal，强调二者的不同。

### Q48. The gardens of the homeless, which are in effect homeless gardens, introduce form into an urban environment where it either didn’t exist or was not discernible as such.

**参考译文/解析**: 本句考查定语从句和固定搭配。which are in effect homeless gardens 是一个非限制性定语从句，需要灵活处理。where 引导的定语从句修饰 environment，理解 it either didn’t exist or was not discernible as such 是关键。

### Q49. most of us give in to a demoralization of spirit which we usually blame on some psychological conditions, until one day we find ourselves in a garden and feel the oppression vanish as if by magic.

**参考译文/解析**: 本句考查定语从句、状语从句和固定搭配。理解 give in to a demoralization of spirit 的含义是关键，以及 until 引导的时间状语从句的作用。

### Q50. It is this implicit or explicit reference to nature that fully justifies the use of the word garden, though in a “liberated” sense, to describe these synthetic constructions.

**参考译文/解析**: 本句考查强调句型、插入式让步状语和不定式做目的状语。It is...that... 是强调句型，需要突出强调。though in a “liberated” sense 是插入的让步状语，需要灵活处理。reference to nature是关键。
