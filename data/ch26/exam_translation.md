### Q46. The growth of the use of English as the world's primary language for international communication has obviously been continuing for several decades.

**参考译文/解析**: 本句主语是 The growth of the use of English as the world's primary language for international communication，谓语是 has been continuing，时间状语是 for several decades。翻译时将 the use of English 翻译为“英语的使用”，primary language 为“主要语言”，international communication 为“国际交流”。

### Q47. His analysis should therefore end any self-contentedness among those who may believe that the global position of English is so stable that the young generations of the United Kingdom do not need additional language capabilities.

**参考译文/解析**: 本句是一个复杂的句子，包含多个从句。主句是His analysis should therefore end any self-contentedness among those。among those后面接了一个定语从句who may believe that the global position of English is so stable that...。so...that结构表示如此...以至于...。翻译时，可以采用顺译法，将定语从句翻译成“那些认为...的人”。

### Q48. many countries are introducing English into the primary-school curriculum but British schoolchildren and students do not appear to be gaining greater encouragement to achieve fluency in other languages.

**参考译文/解析**: 本句是一个对比句，使用but连接两个分句。many countries are introducing English into the primary-school curriculum是一个主谓宾结构的简单句。but后面的分句British schoolchildren and students do not appear to be gaining greater encouragement to achieve fluency in other languages. 同样是主谓宾结构，be gaining encouragement to do sth表示“得到鼓励去做某事”。

### Q49. The English language teaching sector directly earns nearly ￡1.3 billion for the UK in invisible exports and our other education related exports earn up to ￡10 billion a year more.

**参考译文/解析**: 这是一个并列句，用and连接两个分句。The English language teaching sector directly earns nearly ￡1.3 billion for the UK in invisible exports是主句，主语是The English language teaching sector，谓语是earns，宾语是￡1.3 billion，for the UK和in invisible exports都是状语。and后的分句结构类似。翻译的时候，可以按照顺序翻译。

### Q50. It gives a basis to all organisations which seek to promote the learning and use of English, a basis for planning to meet the possibilities of what could be a very different operating environment. That is a necessary and practical approach. In this as in much else, those who wish to influence the future must prepare for it.

**参考译文/解析**: 这是一个复合句。主句是It gives a basis to all organisations。which seek to promote the learning and use of English是定语从句，修饰organisations。a basis for planning to meet the possibilities of what could be a very different operating environment是a basis的同位语，解释说明basis的具体内容。第二句简单，that指代前一句的内容。第三句，In this as in much else是一个插入语，可以翻译成“在这方面以及在其他许多方面一样”。those who wish to influence the future是一个主语从句，must prepare for it是谓语动词。
