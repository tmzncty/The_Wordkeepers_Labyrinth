### Q46. It was also, and this is unknown even to many people well read about the period, a battle between those who made codes and those who broke them.

**参考译文/解析**: 本句主要考察定语从句和固定搭配。`well read about the period` 是一个形容词短语作后置定语，修饰 `people`。`those who made codes and those who broke them` 指的是编码者和译码者。翻译时注意语序调整，先翻译主干，再翻译定语。

---

### Q47. It listed many documents in code that had been captured from the French army of Spain, and whose secrets had been revealed by the work of one George Scovell, an officer in British headquarters.

**参考译文/解析**: 本句包含两个定语从句，修饰 `documents in code`。`that had been captured from the French army of Spain` 说明文件的来源，`whose secrets had been revealed by the work of one George Scovell, an officer in British headquarters` 说明文件被破译的情况。翻译时注意将定语从句处理成合适的修饰成分。

### Q48. he could not analyze carefully what this obscure officer may or may not have contributed to that great struggle between nations or indeed tell us anything much about the man himself.

**参考译文/解析**: 本句主要考察宾语从句。`what this obscure officer may or may not have contributed to that great struggle between nations` 是 `analyze` 的宾语从句。`tell us anything much about the man himself` 是 `tell` 的双宾语结构。`may or may not` 表示不确定性。翻译时注意语序的调整。

---

### Q49. There may have been many spies and intelligence officers during the Napoleonic Wars, but it is usually extremely difficult to find the material they actually provided or worked on.

**参考译文/解析**: 本句考察存在句和形式主语。`There may have been...` 表达一种推测性的存在。`it is usually extremely difficult to find the material...` 是一个形式主语结构，真正的主语是 `to find the material...`。`they actually provided or worked on` 是一个定语从句，修饰 `the material`。翻译时注意语序的调整和词义的选择。

---

### Q50. Just as the code breaking has its wider relevance in the struggle for Spain, so his attempts to make his way up the promotion ladder speak volumes about British society.

**参考译文/解析**: 本句考察 `just as...so...` 结构，表达类比关系。`his attempts to make his way up the promotion ladder` 是一个名词短语，`to make his way up the promotion ladder` 是不定式作后置定语，修饰 `attempts`。`speak volumes about British society` 是一个比喻，表示充分说明了英国社会的现实。翻译时注意结构和词义的准确表达。
