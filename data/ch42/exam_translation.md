### Q46. AI can also be used to identify the lifestyle choices of customers regarding their hobbies, favourite celebrities, and fashions to provide unique content in marketing messages put out through social media.

**参考译文/解析**: 本句的主干是AI can be used to identify lifestyle choices to provide content。regarding...fashions作lifestyle choices的后置定语。in marketing messages put out through social media是介词短语作后置定语修饰content。

---

### Q47. Some believe that AI is negatively impacting on the marketer’s role by reducing creativity and removing jobs, but they are aware that it is a way of reducing costs and creating new information.

**参考译文/解析**: 本句是由but连接的并列句。前一个分句中，that AI is negatively impacting on the marketer’s role是believe的宾语从句，by reducing creativity and removing jobs是方式状语。后一个分句中，it is a way of reducing costs and creating new information是主系表结构。

---

### Q48. Algorithms used to simulate human interactions are creating many of these concerns, especially as no-one is quite sure what the outcomes of using AI to interact with customers will be.

**参考译文/解析**: 本句的主干是Algorithms are creating concerns。used to simulate human interactions是过去分词短语作后置定语修饰Algorithms。as引导原因状语从句，其中what the outcomes of using AI to interact with customers will be是从句中的主语从句。

---

### Q49. If customers are not willing to share data, AI will be starved of essential  information and will not be able to function effectively or employ machine learning to improve its marketing content and communication.

**参考译文/解析**: 本句是由if引导的条件状语从句。主句部分是由and连接的并列谓语will not be able to function effectively or employ machine learning to improve its marketing content and communication。

---

### Q50. The non-intrusive delivery of the marketing messages in a way that is sensitive to the needs of target customers is one of the critical challenges to the digital marketer.

**参考译文/解析**: 本句的主干是The delivery is one of the challenges。non-intrusive和sensitive to the needs of target customers分别修饰delivery。in a way that is sensitive to the needs of target customers作定语修饰delivery。
