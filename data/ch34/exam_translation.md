# Ch. — 翻译 (Translation)

> **年份**: 2020 | **总分**: 10分

---

### Q46. with the Church’s teachings and ways of thinking eclipsed by the Renaissance,the gap between the Medieval and modern periods had been bridged leading to new and unexplored intellectual territories.

**参考译文/解析**: 本句是由with引导的伴随状语结构，主句是the gap...had been bridged，现在分词leading...作结果状语。翻译时可调整语序，先翻译主句，再翻译with引导的状语，最后翻译结果状语。

---

### Q47. Before each of their revelations many thinkers at the time had sustained more ancient ways of thinking, including the geocentric view that the Earth was at the centre of our universe.

**参考译文/解析**: 本句的主干是many thinkers…had sustained more ancient ways of thinking。before each of their revelations作时间状语，including the geocentric view…是ways of thinking的同位语，that the Earth was at the centre of our universe是the geocentric view 的同位语从句。注意revelations是“启示，揭示”的意思，不能翻译成“革命”。sustained在此处是“坚持，维持”的意思。

---

### Q48. Despite attempts by the Church to suppress. this new generation of logicians and rationalists, more explanations for how the universe functioned were being made at a rate that the people could no longer ignore.

**参考译文/解析**: 本句是一个由despite引导的让步状语从句，主句是more explanations…were being made…that the people could no longer ignore 是一个定语从句，修饰rate。翻译时可以将语序调整为：关于宇宙如何运转的更多解释正以人们无法忽视的速度涌现，尽管教会试图压制新一代的逻辑学家和理性主义者。

---

### Q49. As many took on the duty of trying to integrate reasoning and scientific philosophies into the world, the Renaissance was over and it was time for a new era - the Age of Reason.

**参考译文/解析**: 本句是由as引导的时间状语从句，主句是the Renaissance was over and it was time for a new era。注意integrate…into…是“将…融入…”的意思。翻译时可以按照原文的语序进行翻译。

---

### Q50. Such actions to seek knowledge and to understand what information we already knew were captured by the Latin phrase ‘sapere aude’ or ‘dare to know’, after Immanuel Kant used it in his essay An Answer to the Question: What is Enlightenment?.

**参考译文/解析**: 本句主干是Such actions…were captured by the Latin phrase ‘sapere aude’。to seek knowledge and to understand what information we already knew作定语修饰actions，after Immanuel Kant used it in his essay…作后置状语，解释拉丁短语的来源。注意‘sapere aude’ or ‘dare to know’是同位语，对拉丁文短语进行解释。
