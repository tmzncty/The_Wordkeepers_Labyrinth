---

### Q46. Recent  decades  have  seen  more  engagement  in  the subject can only be done through institutions such as a university.

**参考译文/解析**: 本句的主干是Recent decades have seen...。宾语是more engagement in the subject。that引导的从句修饰subject，that在从句中作主语。only be done through institutions such as a university是定语从句。

---

### Q47. But by utilising the natural curiosity of the general public it is possible to overcome many of these challenges  by  engaging  non-scientists  directly  in  the  research  process.

**参考译文/解析**: 本句是一个由but连接的并列句。主句主语是由it作形式主语，真正的主语是to overcome many of these challenges。by utilising...和by engaging...都是方式状语。

---

### Q48. Scientists have employed a variety of ways to engage the general public in their research,such as making data analysis into an online game or  sample  collection  into   a  smartphone  application.

**参考译文/解析**: 本句主干是Scientists have employed a variety of ways to engage the general public in their research。such as 引导的是举例说明，具体说明了科学家们采用的各种方式。

---

### Q49. These  groups  of people are part of a rapidly expanding biotechnological social movement of citizen scientists and professional scientists seeking to take discovery out of institutions and put it into the hands of anyone with the enthusiasm.

**参考译文/解析**: 本句主干是These groups of people are part of a rapidly expanding biotechnological social movement。of citizen scientists and professional scientists是movement的同位语。seeking to take discovery out of institutions and put it into the hands of anyone with the enthusiasm是现在分词短语作后置定语，修饰scientists.

---

### Q50. They pool  resources,collaborate,think  outside  the  box,and  find  solutions and

**参考译文/解析**: 本句是由and连接的并列谓语结构。pool resources, collaborate, think outside the box, and find solutions 是四个并列的动词。
